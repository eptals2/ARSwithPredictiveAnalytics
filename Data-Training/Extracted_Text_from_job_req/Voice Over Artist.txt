Voice Over Artist
Job Description:
We are seeking 15 talented Filipino Voice Over Artist to record high-quality audio in Filipino.These recordings will be used to train AI models for voice recognition and natural language processing, enhancing the AI's understanding of Filipino accents and nuances.
Responsibilities:
Record clear, professional audio in Filipino.
Ensure consistency in tone, pronunciation, and pacing.
Collaborate with the AI team to meet project requirements and deadlines.
Requirements:
Native or fluent Filipino speaker.
Experience in voice-over, narration, or a similar field.
Ability to deliver high-quality audio recordings.
Access to professional recording equipment (microphone, soundproof setup, etc.).
Next Steps:
The process is simple: initially, we need a 2-minute sample voice-over in Filipino along with the script, to check the audio quality. The audio must be in WAV format (48kHz, Mono, 24-bit). If you already have a 2-minute sample in your native language, you can share it, provided it meets the audio specifications. Send your sample and script to sagar.gupta@truelancer.com.


Once we confirm the quality, you can either record 1 hour and 30 minutes of audio with scripts or, if you have a pre-recorded audio with scripts that you hold the rights to, we may be able to purchase those as well. Just ensure the audio is in WAV format (48kHz, Mono, 24-bit) and perfectly aligned with the script.
