Digital Marketing Assistant
Key Responsibilities:
As a Digital Marketing Intern, you will have the opportunity to explore various facets of the business. The specific tasks assigned will depend on current projects and priorities, but here are some sample responsibilities you may be involved in:


Digital Media Ads: Assist in the setup, monitoring, and optimization of digital advertising campaigns on platforms such as Google Ads, Meta, and others.
Reporting and Analytics: Analyze campaign performance, create detailed reports, and provide actionable insights to improve marketing strategies.
Social Media Content: Support in creating and scheduling posts for brand social media pages, engaging with audiences, and helping manage online communities.
Influencer Marketing: Help identify potential influencers, coordinate outreach, and track campaign results.
Content Creation: Collaborate on the development of digital assets such as blogs, videos, graphics, or other promotional materials.
Email Marketing: Contribute to creating and executing email marketing campaigns while maintaining customer databases.
Market Research: Conduct research on industry trends, competitors, and customer preferences to support strategic decision-making.
Administrative Support: Provide general support to the digital marketing team to ensure seamless project execution.


Who We’re Looking For:
Fresh Graduates & Career Starters Welcome: No prior experience is necessary, just a passion for learning and digital marketing.
Eagerness to Learn: A proactive attitude and the drive to take on new challenges.
Smart & Hardworking: Problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a strong work ethic.
Familiarity with social media platforms, digital tools, or analytics is a bonus but not required.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
A degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field is a plus but not mandatory.
Creativity and a willingness to think outside the box.
