Client Partner Philippines (Contract)

About the job
We are looking for a Client Partner to join our Advertising Sales team to manage our sales initiatives in the Philippines.


We want someone who loves our product and wakes up every morning happy to interact with our clients. You should be confident without ever being arrogant. You work hard, but know how to have fun. You should also exhibit a growth mindset that can rise above any challenges. Most importantly, your work will impact the way brands connect with the Spotify users!


This is a 12 month fixed term contract role based in Manila, Philippines, reporting to the Director of Sales based in Singapore.


What You'll Do
Focus on brand promotion, educating and inspiring our clients to use Spotify to enhance their business
Promote display advertising (video, mobile, programmatic), audio formats, and other ad space in order to support the advertising business by building, sustaining and growing a dedicated book of business with media agencies, creative agencies and clients directly
Work across multiple agency departments, i.e. programmatic, offline and digital, branded content and innovation teams, to assist with delivering successful and effective campaigns
Monitor and execute potential upsell opportunities
Partner with internal marketing, brand partnerships, and label relations teams to maximize earnings for Spotify and our partners
Conduct industry analysis and preparing relevant promotion and customers materials
Respond to RFPs and deliver extraordinary media proposals
Work with Account and Campaign Management team to monitor campaign performance, recommend optimisations, and support campaign delivery
Represent Spotify at promotional events
Diligent in managing all activity using salesforce.com on a regular basis and reporting your patch of business


Who You Are
Diploma or Bachelor’s Degree, preferably in Advertising, Business, Communications or Marketing
A minimum of 5+ years of experience in either media agency, media owner/publisher, client management or sales role
You have a strong knowledge of the Philippines digital media marketplace and the existing relationship with all levels of the agencies; direct brands will be a major plus point
Knowledge of online and offline media planning and buying ecosystem including programmatic advertising and have the ability to be creative with audio and digital client solutions
You are obsessed with customer satisfaction with a proven track record to build internal and external relationships
Ability to deliver on multiple priorities with attention to detail despite tight deadlines
Analytical with a consistent record to think strategically and creatively
You have dexterity in creating reports and presentations using Excel, PowerPoint and Keynote; experience of Salesforce, DSM and DFP is a plus
A strong team player, smart, adapts well with change and has a positive attitude
You have strong negotiation skills and are able present effectively and close the deal
You are able to work in a fast-paced, exciting environment and across external and internal teams to deliver creative, fresh campaigns.
Proficiency in English & Tagalog (written & spoken)
