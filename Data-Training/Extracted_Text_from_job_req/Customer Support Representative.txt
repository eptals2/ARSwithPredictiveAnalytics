Customer Support Representative (Non-voice)
Job Description:
We are looking for an experienced Customer Service Representative with 2 years of non-voice chat support experience. In this role, you will provide high-quality customer support through live chat, assisting customers with inquiries, resolving issues, and ensuring satisfaction.


Key Responsibilities:
Provide real-time assistance to customers via live chat.
Address customer inquiries, resolve complaints, and offer product/service information.
Maintain accurate records of customer interactions and follow up when needed.
Ensure all customer issues are resolved promptly and professionally.
Meet performance targets and maintain high customer satisfaction levels.
Collaborate with other teams to resolve complex issues and improve service.


Qualifications:
2 years of experience in non-voice chat support or similar customer service role.
Excellent written communication skills.
Strong problem-solving and multitasking abilities.
Ability to handle multiple chat conversations efficiently.
Familiarity with customer service platforms or CRM systems is a plus.
Must be willing to work 100% onsite in Double Dragon Plaza, Pasay City.


If you're a skilled communicator with a passion for customer service and live chat support, we'd love for you to join our team!
