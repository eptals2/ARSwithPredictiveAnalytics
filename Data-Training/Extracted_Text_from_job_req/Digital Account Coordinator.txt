Digital Account Coordinator

Job Description


As a Digital Account Coordinator, you will work closely with Account Managers to execute impactful digital campaigns. From preparing reports to ensuring clients’ advertising success, you’ll play a pivotal role in delivering superior customer service and campaign excellence.

The Qualifications We Seek


Experience in the digital advertising company, preferably from Metro Manila is a must.
At least 2-5 years of experience in digital account coordination.
1-2 years of experience in digital marketing, preferably in a full-time capacity.
Knowledge of social media advertising platforms (Facebook, X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest) and a solid understanding of online marketing.
Proficient in Excel, PowerPoint, Google Sheets, and Google Slides, with attention to detail and accuracy.
Salesforce experience is a plus but not mandatory.
Strong communication and presentation skills, both written and verbal.
Ability to work independently and as part of a distributed team.
Ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
Analytical skills with a proven record of using data to drive decision-making.
Ability to think creatively and solve problems proactively.


Your Daily Tasks


Collaborate with Account Managers to support client advertising campaigns, ensuring flawless execution and superior customer service.
Proactively manage client accounts to increase retention rates through strategic campaign execution.
Help clients assess their advertising success by preparing reports and campaign documentation.
Regularly review and analyze client campaigns, making insightful recommendations to ensure success.
Assist in day-to-day client interactions, ensuring that client needs are met efficiently.
