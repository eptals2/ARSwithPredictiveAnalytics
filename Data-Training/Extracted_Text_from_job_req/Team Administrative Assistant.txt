Team Administrative Assistant

As a Team Administrative Assistant, you will be responsible for drafting various types of correspondence via letter and email. This includes crafting Cover Letters, drafting Division 293 Notice emails, and preparing Land Tax Notice emails and letters. Additionally, you will be tasked with drafting letters such as engagement and ethical letters, as well as handling other client correspondence, including NOA IAS letters.

Preferable experience with Workflow Max and Receipt Bank use 
Experience in Australian accounting, with high-level exposure to Australian GST legislation, is a definite advantage 
Strong communication skills, both written and verbal 
Sound understanding of contemporary Australian business practices and culture 
Excellent client service skills, with the ability to provide professional and responsive service
