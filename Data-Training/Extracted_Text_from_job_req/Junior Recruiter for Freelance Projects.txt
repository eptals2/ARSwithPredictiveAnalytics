Junior Recruiter for Freelance Projects

Job Description


You will identify the best experts among freelancers and development companies for our internal and external customers
Your responsibilities include the acquisition and support of customers in the field of digital solutions
You will be in charge of the process from the first contact with the customer to coordination, offer calculations and the creation and finalization of offers, including the creation, maintenance and management of leads and opps, both for freelancers and customers.
Review of requirements and general conditions for the legally compliant use of freelancers in customer projects
Active sourcing of freelancers - advertising of projects on online platforms, direct contact with freelancers as well as pre-selection and profile creation
First point of contact for freelancers and clients for all administrative issues during the entire duration of the project
Project creation, maintenance and management in various software tools as well as budget monitoring, invoicing and auditing
Structuring, standardization and optimization of existing workflows as well as constant exchange with the development team for digital/automated implementation on the LegendsLab platform.
Interact with marketing to attract talents and customers


Qualifications


University degree
Personality: friendly & empathic, thinking and acting economically, creative, performance-oriented, responsible, able to make decisions, strong communication skills, enthusiastic
Working style: good presentation and moderation skills, analytical ability, strong strength in work organization & prioritization.
Languages: English fluent; German is a plus
