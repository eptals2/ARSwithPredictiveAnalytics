Fine-Tuning Success Every Day


Oversee the post-sale execution of national digital media campaigns, focusing on direct campaigns under $75,000 and programmatic guaranteed deals.
Collaborate with internal departments to ensure programs are booked and executed in alignment with operational and sales goals.
Manage client communications during the setup, launch, flight, and recap phases of both direct and programmatic campaigns across multiple DSPs (e.g., DV360, TTD).
Schedule and lead internal and external calls as needed, ensuring production timelines and deliverables are communicated effectively to exceed client expectations.
Partner with Sales, agencies, third-party measurement vendors, and AdOps teams to troubleshoot campaigns and resolve issues promptly.
Coordinate with pod operations to address creative and tracking challenges.
Maintain and optimize client campaigns to improve performance against predetermined benchmarks on an ongoing basis.
Ensure full delivery of dollars booked across all digital, social, and podcast deliverables.
Collaborate with the billing and finance teams monthly to reconcile billing accurately and on time.
Deliver comprehensive post-campaign wrap-ups, including final reports and one-sheet recaps with insights and learnings.
