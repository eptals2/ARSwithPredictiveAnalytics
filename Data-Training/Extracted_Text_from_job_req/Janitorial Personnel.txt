Janitorial Personnel
Job Description:
- Perform routine cleaning tasks, including sweeping, mopping, dusting, and vacuuming.
- Sanitize restrooms.
- Dispose of waste properly and ensure garbage bins are regularly emptied.
- Maintain cleanliness in office spaces, hallways, and other designated areas.
- Report any maintenance or repair needs, such as leaks or broken fixtures.
- Operate cleaning equipment safely and efficiently.
- Follow health, safety, and sanitation guidelines set by the company.
- Request necessary items and materials to carry out janitorial activities effectively.
Qualifications:
- Atleast High School Level.
- Ability to work independently and efficiently.
- Physically capable of handling cleaning tasks and lifting moderate weights.
- Hardworking, reliable, and punctual.
